v2.4.2 — Biometric robustness after fingerprint re-enrollment

* Detects when the Keystore key has been invalidated by an Android
  fingerprint re-enrollment. Before: the biometric button showed
  "Biometric failure" without guidance. Now: the wrap is auto-wiped,
  and a clear message invites the user to unlock with the master
  password and then re-enable biometric in Settings.
* Confirmation snack on biometric enable AND disable in Settings
  (used to be silent).
* Distinct snack when biometric activation is canceled by the user
  (back / cancel gesture) or fails at the hardware level.
* New typed UnlockResult.biometricInvalidated to stop swallowing
  biometric_storage exceptions silently.
